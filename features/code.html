Frequently Asked Questions

Have a different question? Reach out to our support team by sending us an email and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

What type of content can I use to train the chatbot? expand_more
You can train your chatbot using website URLs, uploaded files (PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV), or raw text pasted directly into the dashboard.
How long does training take? expand_more
Most sites finish training within a few minutes. Larger sites with hundreds of pages may take up to an hour.
Does it retrain automatically when my website changes? expand_more
Yes. Depending on your plan, you can set up automatic monthly, weekly, or daily re-syncs so your chatbot always reflects the latest content.
How do I add the chatbot to my website? expand_more
Each chatbot gets a unique embed code. Paste the script tag before the closing </body> on any site and the widget appears instantly — no developer required.
Can I upload files to train the chatbot? expand_more
Yes, you can upload PDFs, Word documents, plain text files, and CSVs directly from your dashboard.
Can I embed the chatbot on multiple websites? expand_more
Yes, a single chatbot can be embedded on as many websites or apps as you'd like at no extra cost.
Can I add raw text content if I don't have web pages to scrape? expand_more
Yes. You can paste raw text content directly into the dashboard and use it as training data for your chatbot.
Can I customize the chatbot's appearance? expand_more
Yes, you can customize the chatbot's name, avatar, colors, welcome message, and quick prompts to match your brand.
What AI models does Slabscan AI use? expand_more
We support GPT-4o and GPT-4.1. GPT-4o prioritizes speed and cost-efficiency, while GPT-4.1 provides deeper reasoning and accuracy for complex queries.
Does it support multiple languages? expand_more
Yes, the chatbot understands and responds in over 95 languages automatically based on the visitor's question — no extra configuration needed.
Can the chatbot escalate to a human agent? expand_more
Yes. When the chatbot can't resolve an issue, users can seamlessly request a handoff to a live human agent. You can configure escalation triggers and route to Zendesk, email, or a custom channel.
What are Quick Prompts? expand_more
Quick Prompts are pre-set conversation starters displayed to visitors when they open the chatbot. They help users immediately find value and reduce friction for common questions.
Will I be able to see the chats users are having with my chatbot? expand_more
Yes, every conversation is logged in your dashboard so you can review what visitors asked and how the chatbot responded.
What are Email Summaries? expand_more
Daily digest emails that summarize your chatbot's activity: total chats, top questions, unanswered queries, and suggestions for new training content.
Can I use the chatbot for lead generation? expand_more
Yes. Configure the chatbot to collect visitor name, email, and company at the right moment in the conversation. Leads are exported to your CRM or emailed to you directly.
Do you offer a whitelabel option? expand_more
Yes, whitelabeling — removing our branding and adding your own — is available on our agency and Enterprise plans.
Is my data secure? expand_more
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, access is role-restricted, and we never use your data to train AI models.
Are you HIPAA compliant? expand_more
Yes. We have been assessed for HIPAA compliance, and Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) are available for Enterprise plan customers.
Are you GDPR compliant? expand_more
Yes. We are GDPR compliant and support data subject access, export, and deletion requests for EU users.
What is SOC 2 Type II? expand_more
SOC 2 Type II is an independent audit that verifies our security controls covering data security, availability, and confidentiality over an extended period — not just a point in time.
Where is my data stored? expand_more
Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest. Enterprise customers can request a specific data region.
Do you have a Data Processing Agreement (DPA)? expand_more
Yes, a standard DPA is available on request for any customer that needs one for compliance purposes.
Is my training data used to train AI models? expand_more
No. Your content is never used to train shared or public AI models. It is only used to power your specific chatbot.
Do you offer Role-Based Access Control? expand_more
Yes. You can add team members with specific roles — some can view conversation history, others can manage training content, and admins have full control.
Do you have an API? expand_more
Yes. Our REST API lets you manage chatbots, training sources, and conversations programmatically.
Does it integrate with Slack? expand_more
Yes, install the Slack integration to ask your chatbot questions directly from any Slack channel or DM.
Does it integrate with Zendesk? expand_more
Yes. The Zendesk integration lets the chatbot handle tier-1 support queries and escalate directly to your Zendesk ticketing queue when needed.
Is WhatsApp supported? expand_more
Yes, WhatsApp Business integration is available so your chatbot can reply directly inside WhatsApp chats.
Does it integrate with Crisp or Intercom? expand_more
Yes. We have native integrations with both Crisp and Intercom that let our AI auto-draft or auto-send replies inside those tools.
Can I connect Slabscan AI to my CRM? expand_more
Yes. Using our API and webhooks, you can push leads, chat summaries, and conversation data to HubSpot, Salesforce, or any CRM that accepts incoming webhooks.
Does it support Facebook Messenger? expand_more
Yes, connect your chatbot to a Facebook Page in a few clicks and it will answer Messenger conversations automatically.
My company needs a custom integration. What should I do? expand_more
Reach out to our support team with the details and we'll evaluate building it as a custom integration on our Enterprise plan.
