Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question that's not answered here? Email our support team and we'll get back to you.

Is there a free plan? expand_more
We offer a 7-day free trial on all paid plans so you can fully test the chatbot before committing — no credit card required.
Can I embed a chatbot on multiple websites? expand_more
Yes, a single chatbot can be embedded on as many websites or apps as you'd like at no extra cost.
What is considered a "message"? expand_more
A message is counted each time the chatbot generates a reply to a visitor. Visitor questions themselves are not counted.
What is a "page" in the context of training limits? expand_more
A "page" is any single URL, uploaded file, or block of raw text used as a training source — each one counts toward your plan's page limit.
Do you offer annual discounts? expand_more
Yes! All prices shown are the annual rate. Paying yearly saves you roughly 2 months compared to monthly billing.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? expand_more
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately and are prorated.
Do you have a plan for agencies? expand_more
Yes, our agency tier includes multi-client management, usage pooling, and reseller pricing. Contact us for details.
What happens if I exceed my message limit? expand_more
You'll receive an email notification when you approach your limit. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional messages to avoid any interruption.
Is there an enterprise plan? expand_more
Yes, our Enterprise plan offers white-label chatbots, dedicated account management, custom SLAs, SSO, and volume pricing. Contact sales for a custom quote.
What payment methods do you accept? expand_more
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) through Stripe. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice.
What is the refund policy? expand_more
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on your first payment. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
Does the free trial require a credit card? expand_more
No credit card is required to start your 7-day free trial. You only need to add payment info if you decide to continue after the trial.
