Can I add text content to train the chatbot if I don't have web pages to scrape or files to upload? 💬 Ask expand_more
Yes. If you don't have web pages or files, you can paste raw text content directly into the dashboard and use it as training data for your chatbot.
Do you have a plan for agencies to offer chatbots to their clients? 💬 Ask expand_more
Yes. Our agency plan lets you create and manage chatbots for multiple clients under one account, with whitelabeling available on higher tiers.
Is there a demo that I can try? 💬 Ask expand_more
Yes, scroll up and try the live chatbot demo on this page, or start a free trial to build your own.
What type of content can we use to train the chatbot? 💬 Ask expand_more
You can train your chatbot using website URLs, uploaded files (PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV), or raw text pasted directly into the dashboard.
Do you retrain the chatbot automatically when the website content changes? 💬 Ask expand_more
Yes, depending on your plan you can set up automatic monthly, weekly, or daily re-syncs so your chatbot always reflects the latest content.
Can I upload files to train the chatbot? 💬 Ask expand_more
Yes, you can upload PDFs, Word documents, plain text files, and CSVs directly from your dashboard.
Some of my files are larger than 10 MB. What do I do? 💬 Ask expand_more
Split larger files into smaller parts before uploading, or reach out to support and we can help raise your file size limit.
How do I add the chatbot to my website? 💬 Ask expand_more
Each chatbot gets a unique embed code. Paste the script tag before the closing </body> on any site and the widget appears instantly.
How do I train the chatbot? 💬 Ask expand_more
Add a URL to scan, upload files, or paste raw text into the training panel. The chatbot indexes the content automatically once added.
How long does the training take? 💬 Ask expand_more
Most sites finish training within a few minutes. Larger sites with hundreds of pages may take up to an hour.
How can we contact you? 💬 Ask expand_more
You can reach our support team anytime at support@slabscan.ai, or use the live chatbot on this page.
Can you embed a chatbot on multiple websites? 💬 Ask expand_more
Yes, a single chatbot can be embedded on as many websites or apps as you'd like at no extra cost.
Do you have a plan for agencies to offer chatbots to their clients? 💬 Ask expand_more
Yes, our agency tier includes multi-client management, usage pooling, and reseller pricing.
Is there a free plan? 💬 Ask expand_more
We offer a 7-day free trial on all paid plans so you can fully test the chatbot before committing, no credit card required.
Is there a demo that I can try? 💬 Ask expand_more
Yes, try the live chatbot demo above on this page to see exactly how it answers real questions.
What is a "page" in the context of training limits? 💬 Ask expand_more
A "page" is any single URL, uploaded file, or block of raw text used as a training source — each one counts toward your plan's page limit.
Is there a limit on number of messages that can be sent? 💬 Ask expand_more
Each plan includes a monthly message allowance. If you go over, you can upgrade your plan or purchase additional message packs.
Does it work on websites that are in other languages than English? 💬 Ask expand_more
Yes, the chatbot understands and responds in over 95 languages automatically based on the visitor's question.
